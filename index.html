A working hypothesis

What if the average user is the wrong unit?

If ~5% of payers really drive ~95% of revenue — a benchmark I'm assuming from the outside, not something I have internal data on — then one reasonable response might be to treat this more like fund allocation than a dashboard: test cheaply and cut early in the lab, but be slower to cancel a saga once it ships, since a whale who's invested dozens of episodes probably won't come back from a cancelled story. And because acquisition looks expensive, it could be worth scaling on the cost to land a whale (WAC) rather than the blended average. The rest of this is me trying to pressure-test that guess.
